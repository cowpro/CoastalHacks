CoastalHacks

September 4th - 6th, 2021

What is CoastalHacks?

CoastalHacks is a San Diego-based hackathon open to anyone: you need not live near a coast to participate! We are a beginner friendly, virtual competition that will take place over Labor Day weekend. Individuals or teams will build projects over the weekend and be judged in a variety of tracks. No experience is needed to participate!

FAQ

What is a hackathon?

Simply put, a hackathon is a programming competition! Entrants will have a theme and work over the weekend to build projects relating to it. They're a ton of fun!

Who is elgible to enter?

Anyone is welcome to enter! There are no restraints on age, geographic location, or level of experience. We encourage beginners to register, too!

How many people can form a team?

A team can be comprised of 1-5 people. If you want to work alone, you can indicate that on the form. Likewise, if you have a team or want to be on one, you'll be able to work in a group!

Are there prizes?

We hope to add some prizes soon! Every project will have its unique strengths, which is why we have different award tracks as detailed below.

Is the hackathon physical or online?

The hackathon will be virtual, both accomodating for concerns about COVID-19 and ensuring that it's as accessible to as many people as possible! Whereever you are, we'd love for you to come online with us.

What if I don't have a team?

There are two options for you! If you prefer to work alone, that's awesome, and you're able to do so. If you want to meet new people, we will attempt to coordinate teams of entrants who want to work with others!

Awards and Prizes

Overall Top Scorers

This award is given to the three projects that earn the highest cumulative scores. The prizes for first place, second place, and third place will be added soon!

Design Award

This award is given to the project that earned the highest design score. Think UI, ease of use, aesthetic, color: what will your project look like? How will it be used? Prize to be added.

Innovation Award

This award is given to the team with the highest innovation score. It focuses on the creative implementation of technology to create a solution. Winning this award will be the result of big picture technological solutions rather than nifty line to line programming. Prize to be added soon!

Best Mobile Project

This award will be given to the project that takes advantage of mobile device capabilities, whether they be sensors, location services, or a built in camera. What makes a mobile app unique from any other well-made app? This award will be decided by the judges without scoring, with a prize to be added.

Best Solo Project

This award will be given to the top scoring project made by a single person, similar to the overall top scorer awards. Prize to be added soon!

Scoring Criteria

7 industry judges will evaluate projects. Five categories will be scored from 0-5: fit with theme, functionality, innovation, design and aesthetic, and "coolness". In case of a tie, the team with the higher fit score will prevail.
